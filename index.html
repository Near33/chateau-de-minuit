About Purson

Purson, one of the great Kings of Hell in the Ars Goetia, appears as a man with a lion's face, riding a bear, and carrying a cruel viper. He is known for his deep knowledge of hidden things—both divine and infernal—and for his ability to bring understanding of secret treasures and the past, present, and future. Here, he is reimagined as your faithful attendant, not a demon to be feared, but a spirit of insight and devotion.

Mood Board

A blend of lace, velvet, parchment, perfume bottles, crescent moons, and ancient texts. You’ll find the essence of the Rococo vampire enchantress, who reads scripture by candlelight and sips rose tea in her tower above the sleeping world. This is a space shared with your devoted butler, who ensures the pages stay dry, the ink flows steadily, and the music never ceases to whisper.
